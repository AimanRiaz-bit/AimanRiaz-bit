# Week 1 — Reflection

## Topic Covered: Role of a Computer Science Graduate

## What I Learned

This week I learned that a Computer Science graduate is not just a 
programmer, but also a problem solver and a responsible professional. 
I understood how CS graduates help build systems that are used in 
everyday life, such as in banking and education. The lecture also 
stressed the importance of ethics and the need for continuous learning 
throughout a professional career.

## Key Insight

Having strong technical skills is not enough on its own. The way we 
behave and the sense of responsibility we carry are just as important 
as the code we write.

## Real-Life Application

I can apply this by not only focusing on improving my coding skills, 
but also by working on my problem-solving ability and developing a 
stronger sense of ethical thinking in everything I do.

# Week 3 — Reflection

## Topic Covered: Workplace Culture

## What I Learned

This week I learned about professional behavior, teamwork, diversity, 
and inclusion in the workplace. The lecture helped me understand that 
respect, fairness, and working together are key elements of any 
professional environment. These values are not just nice to have, they 
are essential for a healthy and productive workplace.

## Key Insight

A positive and supportive work environment leads to better productivity 
and encourages creativity and innovation. When people feel respected and 
included, they perform better and contribute more effectively to the team.

## Real-Life Application

I can apply these lessons in my group projects by genuinely respecting 
the opinions of others and making sure I am always contributing in a 
cooperative and helpful way. These habits will also prepare me for a 
professional work setting in the future.
